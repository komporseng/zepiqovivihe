Carolina Herrera Biography

Carolina Herrera is best known as Actress who has an estimated Net Worth of $130 Million. Venezuelan fashion designer and entrepreneur who founded her self-titled label in 1980. She has dressed celebrities such as Michelle Obama and Jacqueline Onassis.  She was born on January 8,1939, Caracas, Venezuela. She became a passionate humanitarian and has served as a Goodwill Ambassador for the Intergovernmental Institution for the use of Micro-Algae Spirulina Against Malnutrition (IIMSAM). She was always fascinated with clothing design and often created outfits for her dolls as a child. She has numerous celebrity fans, including Oscar-winning actress Renee Zellweger. She married Guillermo Behrens Tello in 1957, and, in 1968, she married Reinaldo Herrera Guevara. She has four daughters named Carolina, Patricia, Ana, and Mercedes. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn
