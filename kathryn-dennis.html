Kathryn Calhoun Dennis aka Kathryn Dennis is a television personality and model best known for her Bravo TV reality show, Southern Charm, which she co-hosts with Naomie Olindo. Kathryn Dennis’s Childhood, Biography, and Education

She was born on August 5, 1992, in Charleston, South Carolina, to Luke Dennis (father) and Allison Dennis (mother). Kathryn Calhoun Dennis is her given name. Career  of Kathryn Dennis

She was already a well-trained political personality at the age of 23 after receiving advice from an experienced politician. She made her acting debut as a lead in Southern Charm in 2014.
