Who Are Bachelor's Susie Evans Parents, Tom And Jean Beans Evans?

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  743 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

After the elimination of Serene Russell who secured fourth place, Susie Evans has reached the final three and the finale of the Bachelor 2022. She recently had her hometown date on The Bachelor on 1 March episode. The other two who made it to the finale were Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia.

Now, fans are also interested to learn about Susie’s parents hence we’ve put together this article that covers information related to her parents.

Who Are Susie Evans Parents?

Meet Tom and Jean Beans Evans, parents of Bachelor 26 star Susie Evans. Her parents couldn’t be more proud that she has reached the finale of the Clayton Echard season of the Bachelor. Now learn about her supportive parents below. Here you’ll learn about their age, job, and social media reach.

Plus, we will also discuss the other children that they have.

Meet Susie Evans Dad, Tom Evans

Hailing from Hialeah, Florida, Tom Evans is the father of Susie Evans. He has a brother named Michael Evans who attended Miami Beach High School and worked at Caribbean Cruise Line. He lives in Largo, Florida.

  • Tom Evans Age

Reportedly born in March 1953, Tom Evans is 69 years old. He celebrates his birthday on 13 March.

  • Tom Evans Job

Susie Evans’s dad Tom has been working as a sales manager for Hayward Pool Products since December 1999. Second LinkedIn revealed that he has been working with Goldline Controls as Eastern Regional Sales Manager.

He attended Florida Community College at Jacksonville and earned his Associates’s degree in English and Psychology in 1974. Later, Tom enrolled at Florida Atlantic University in 1976 and earned his bachelor’s degree in English.

  • Is Tom Evans On Facebook?

Yes, Tom Evans is on Facebook. You can find him at (@tom.evans.925) with 439 friends connected with him on the platform.

Meet Susie Evans Mom, Jean Beans Evans

Meet Jean Beans Schellhorn Evans, mother of Susie Evans. Her FB bio reads, “I am a wife, mother, sister, and friend. I help people buy and sell homes in the Hampton Roads Area.”

Jean was born in Chicago, Illinois, and raised in the quiet town of Glen Ellyn just west of the city.

She relocated to Virginia in her 20s as a result of a job promotion.

  • Jean Beans Evans Age

Reportedly born in 1959, Jean Beans Evans turned 62 years old in 2021. She celebrates her birthday on 24 October.

  • Jean Beans Evans Job

Susie Evans’s mom, Jean is a 1980 graduate of the College of DuPage in Special Education. She also earned a BS degree in Special Education and Teaching from Eastern Illinois University in 1982.

Jean started her career as Montgomery Ward/GE Credit in credit and collections from 1986 to 1989. Then, she started working as a Teacher in Norfolk Public Schools from 1989 to 1992. She then served as Library Media Assistant for Poquoson City Schools for five years from 2006 to 2011.

She has been working as a real estate agent at Keller Williams Elite, Peninsula since November 2017 and for Coldwell Banker Professionals, REALTORS since 2014. From 2011 to 2014, Jean also worked in sales and customer service for Stephens Office Supply.

Currently, Jean has been working as a realtor at Coldwell Banker Professional Realtors since 2014 and has been working at Keller Williams Elite, Penninsula since November 2017.

  • Is Jean Beans Evans On Facebook?

Jean Bean Evans is on Facebook (@jeanbeanevans).

  • How Many Kids Do Susie Evans Parents Have?

Besides Susie, Tom and Jean Beans Evans also have a son named Tommy Evans. He is a graduate of Princeton University in 2015. He works as a realtor at Re/Max Capital since July 2018 and Keller Williams Realty. You can read more about his other jobs here.

Tommy is married to Shelbi Evans Metzger who hailed from Brooklyn, Connecticut. She is a master-at-arms at US Army.

Furthermore, she also has a sister named Barbara A Evans.

Susie Evans’s parents currently live in Poquoson, Virginia.
