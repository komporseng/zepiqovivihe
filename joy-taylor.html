Then she came across Fox, which was looking for a host with a great personality and a breadth of experience who could fill in and do a variety of sports. However, the Pennsylvania native made her big break at Fox when she was forced to fill in for Kristine Leahy on one of the episodes of The Herd with Colin Cowherd.

Nobody anticipated that she would become a regular fixture on the show. Taylor was then revealed as the moderator for Fox Sport 1’s debate program, Skip and Shannon: Undisputed, after impressing Fox Sports’ executives.
