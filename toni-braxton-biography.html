Who is Toni Braxton?

Toni Braxton is a singer. She is extremely talented when it comes to acting too. Multi-talented Toni is a pianist and a record producer.

Toni is an incredible personality and a well-known R&B icon. She is 3 times Grammy award-winning artist.

How many siblings does Toni Braxton have?

Toni was born in Severn in the state of Maryland. Born on October 7, 1967. She possesses American nationality and her ethnicity is African-American.

Her birth name is Toni Michelle Braxton. She was born to her parents, Michael Conrad Braxton(father) and Evelyn Jackson(mother).

Her father was a Methodist Clergyman and her mother was an Opera singer.

Her parents were pretty strict about religion. Consequently, she grew up in a religious family. She started singing in the church choir at a young age.

Toni Braxton: Education, School/College University

As per her education, she attended Bowie State University. She thought of getting a teaching degree from there. But she left the college to pursue her singing career.

 Toni Braxton: Professional Life and Career

Toni sang from a young age. She quit college to pursue her singing career professionally. William E. Pettaway, Jr. discovered her after he found her singing in a fuel station.

This changed her life. Although her first single with her sisters was not successful, she moved on positively.

She kept on working hard. Consequently, her debut studio self-titled album made a huge breakthrough in her career.

The album sold more than 10 million copies worldwide. Similarly, Toni Braxton went on to release other albums like, “Secrets”, The Heat”, “Snowflakes”, and “Libra” in later years.

Furthermore, she released more albums like, “Pulse”, “And Love, Marriage & Divorce (with Babyface) part 1 & 2” until now.

As per TV career, she has appeared in multiple shows till now. She appeared in reality TV series, “Braxton Family Values” since 2001 to present.

Discussing her film career, she debuted in a film named, “Kingdom Come”.

Similarly, he did other films like, “The Oogieloves in the Big Balloon Adventure”, “Twist of Faith”, and so on.
