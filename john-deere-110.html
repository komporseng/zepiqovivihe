John Deere, 110 Garden Tractor is equipped with a Kohler K161 gasoline 1-cylinder air-cooled, 277 cc engine, which delivers 7 hp (5.2 kW) power at rated rpm 3600.

John Deere 110 Garden Tractor featured with 7HP Kohler 277cc 1-cyl gasoline or 8HP Kohler 305cc 1-cyl gasoline engine, 4×2 2WD chassis, belt-driven gear with a mechanical CVT transmission and 3 forward and 1 reverse gear.

John Deere 110 Garden Tractor attachments include a 38″ mid-mount mower deck, snowblower, and 42″ blade.
