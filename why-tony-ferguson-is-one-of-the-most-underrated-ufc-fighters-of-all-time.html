Why Tony Ferguson is one of the most underrated UFC Fighters of All-Time

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  499 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Tony Ferguson, a former UFC interim lightweight champion, is widely regarded as one of the most underrated fighters in the history of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). Despite his impressive record and unique fighting style, Ferguson has often been overlooked in discussions about the greatest UFC fighters of all time. In this article, we will explore the reasons why Tony Ferguson deserves more recognition for his contributions to the sport of mixed martial arts (MMA).

Early Career and Transition to UFC

  • Background: Ferguson began his MMA career in 2008 and quickly gained attention for his unorthodox fighting style and exceptional athleticism.

  • The Ultimate Fighter: He gained widespread recognition after winning the 13th season of The Ultimate Fighter, showcasing his resilience and skill against tough competition.

Unconventional Fighting Style

  • Versatility: Ferguson is known for his unorthodox and unpredictable fighting style, incorporating a mix of striking, grappling, and unrelenting pressure.

  • Elusive Footwork: His footwork and movement inside the octagon often confuse opponents, making him difficult to predict and counter.

Impressive Win Streak

  • Record: Ferguson achieved a remarkable 12-fight win streak in the UFC lightweight division, showcasing his dominance and ability to consistently perform at an elite level.

  • Notable Victories: During this streak, he defeated top-ranked opponents such as Rafael dos Anjos, Edson Barboza, and Anthony Pettis, solidifying his position as a top contender.

UFC Interim Lightweight Champion

  • Title Victory: In 2017, Ferguson captured the interim UFC lightweight championship by defeating Kevin Lee, demonstrating his resilience and championship-caliber skills.

  • Champion Mindset: His ability to overcome adversity and secure the interim title showcased his mental toughness and determination.

Setbacks and Resilience

  • Injuries and Setbacks: Despite facing multiple setbacks and injuries throughout his career, Ferguson has consistently bounced back and continued to compete at an elite level.

  • Mental Fortitude: His ability to overcome adversity and maintain a relentless pursuit of greatness exemplifies his resilience as a fighter.

Notable Fight Performances

  • Fight of the Night: Ferguson has been involved in numerous "Fight of the Night" performances, entertaining fans with his aggressive fighting style and willingness to engage in thrilling battles.

  • Exciting Finishes: His ability to secure impressive finishes, including submissions and knockouts, has solidified his reputation as an electrifying fighter.

Overlooked Legacy

  • Comparative Analysis: Despite his exceptional achievements, Ferguson has often been overshadowed by other high-profile UFC fighters, leading to his underrated status within the MMA community.

  • Consistent Performances: His consistent performances and willingness to face top competition make him a deserving candidate for greater recognition.

Tony Ferguson's impact on the sport of MMA and the UFC cannot be overstated. His unorthodox fighting style, impressive win streak, championship success, resilience in the face of setbacks, and entertaining fight performances solidify his place as one of the most underrated UFC fighters of all time. While he may not always receive the same level of attention as other fighters, Ferguson's contributions to the sport and his ability to captivate audiences with his thrilling performances make him a true legend in the world of mixed martial arts.
