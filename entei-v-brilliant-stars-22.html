Entei (Japanese: エンテイV EnteiV) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon V card. It was first released as part of the Brilliant Stars expansion.

Card text

Fleet-Footed
しゅんそく
 
Once during your turn, if this Pokémon is in the Active Spot, you may draw a card.
Burning Rondo
バーニングロンド		20+
This attack does 20 more damage for each Benched Pokémon (both yours and your opponent's).

Release information

This card was included in the Brilliant Stars expansion with artwork by PLANETA Mochizuki, first released in the Japanese Start Deck 100. In Japan, Mirror Holofoil and Non Holofoil versions were available in different decks. It was reprinted, alongside a Galarian Gallery ultra rare card with artwork by Shigenori Negishi, in the Japanese VSTAR Universe subset. The Galarian Gallery ultra rare print was subsequently released outside Asia in the special Crown Zenith expansion.
