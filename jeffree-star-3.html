Career

Jeffree Star started his career as a makeup artist. He used to use fake IDs to attend big Hollywood parties where he received makeup work offers from known celebrities at their homes. After that, he decided to showcase his music and fashion designing talent over social media so he created his account on the popular social media website My Space.

He also used to write blogs about his life, beauty, self-image, and confidence. Soon he accumulated a huge fan following on his account and he becomes one of the most popular independent artists on the application My Space.

After that Jeffree Star started his professional music career and was a part of the True Colors Tour in the year 2007 in which he traveled across 15 cities in the United States. After that, he released his first and only music album titled Beauty Killer in the year 2009.

Since then, Star has released many popular singles and extended plays like Virginity, Mr. Diva, Concealer, Prom Night, and many others.

Along with his music career, Star has been a part of some popular movies as well. He has worked in movies like LA Ink, What Now, Shane and Friends, The Dish With Trish, and many more popular movies.

In the year 2014, Star founded his makeup brand named Jeffree Star Cosmetics.

For the popularity of his cosmetic brand Star started a self-titled YouTube channel on which he uploaded various promotional videos and also gained huge popularity on his channel. His brand has a huge collection of liquid lipsticks, highlighter palettes, lip scrubs, eyeshadow palettes, clothing, and many other accessories.
