Biography/Wiki

Sonia was born on 12 January 1980 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. At a very young age, she decided to become an actress. She has been working in the entertainment industry since the 1990s. She hit the headlines in May 2018 after Himesh Reshammiya posted their marriage pictures with her on social media.

Physical Appearance

She is a beautiful Indian girl who is approximately 5’ 6’’ tall and weighs around 60 kg. She has black eyes and hair.

Family, Caste & Husband

She belongs to a middle-class Khatri family. She is Hindu by religion.

On 11 May 2018, she got married to her long-time boyfriend cum singer & actor Himesh Reshammiya at his Mumbai residence. As per the reports, Sonia and Himesh were dating since 2006 when he was already married to Komal. According to Himesh’s ex-wife, the reason behind their legally part ways is compatibility. Sonia has step-son Swayam (from Himesh’s first wife Komal).

Sonia Kapoor with Himesh Reshammiya

Career

She did her graduation at St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai. After completing studies, she started working as an actress and did numerous TV serials like Kittie Party, Heena (1998-2003), Aa Gale Lag Jaa (2002), Piya Ka Ghar (2002-2006), etc.

Sonia worked in some mythological TV serials like Jai Ganesha as Lakshmi and Jai Hanuman as Mandodari. She has also appeared in several Bollywood films like Officer (2001), Satta (2003), Fareb (2005), Don Muthu Swami (2008), and Karbonn (2015).

Controversies

Allegedly, she has been a reason behind the separation of Himesh Reshammiya and his ex-wife Komal.

Facts
