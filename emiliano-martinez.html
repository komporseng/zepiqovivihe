Damian Emiliano Martinez Romero is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Premier League club Aston Villa and the Argentina national team. He began playing football with the Independiente youth team in 2008 and continued to do so until 2010. He joined Arsenal’s youth team in 2010 and began his career there. On loan, he has previously played for Oxford United, Sheffield Wednesday, Rotherham United, Wolverhampton Wanderers, Getafe, and Reading. Similarly, on September 16, 2020, he signed a four-year contract with rival Premier League club Aston Villa for a price of £20 million. On September 21, 2020, he made his debut for the club.

He has represented Argentina at several age levels on the international stage. He has played for Argentina at the U17, U20, and senior levels. On 4 June 2021, he made his international debut for Argentina in a FIFA World Cup qualification match for 2022. On 14 June 2021, he made his competitive debut in a major event in his team’s opening match at the 2021 Copa America at the Estadio Olimpico Nilton Santos in Brazil. He assisted Argentina in winning the Copa America in 2021.
