Wiki-Bio, wife, kids, life after Little People Big World.

Life can be pretty hard for the little people. Especially if you are raising normal-sized babies. The reality TV series Little People Big World focuses on the livelihood of The Roloff family. The unique family is comprised of dwarves, and the camera mostly focuses on the families’ struggles in raising four children. The series began on the 4th of March 2006 and completed the 24th season in 2023. Jeremy Roloff is one of the non-dwarf siblings of the Roloff family. A regular cast member till 2018, Roloff left the show to follow his new plans. In this article, we will take a closer look at the wiki-bio of Jeremy Roloff.

Wiki/Bio Facts

Early Life

Jeremy Roloff was born on the 10th of May 1990. Born and raised in Oregon, Jeremy is one of the four children of Matthew Roloff and Amy Roloff. As a young kid, Jeremy spent most of his early life with his three siblings, Zach, Molly, and Jacob. Of the three, only his brother Zach has dwarfism. Little is known about Jeremy’s early years. He had a seemingly normal childhood and attended a local high school. After passing his matriculations, Zach attended a college in Santa Barbara, California.

Professional Career

Jeremy’s professional career has always revolved around his family and the TV series, Little People Big World. By the time the series launched in 2006, Jeremy was in his early teens. He had been a regular cast member of the series until 2018. During his time on the show, Jeremy made appearances in most of the episodes and even in two spinoffs: Little People Big World: Wedding Farm (2012), and Little People Big World: Back to the Farm. However, after working all his life on the series, Jeremy decided to jump ship and start a new career with his wife.

“After 14 years, over 300 episodes, and 17 seasons, the time has come. A year ago I made the decision that this season would be our last. It has been an amazing run, and I can’t say thank you enough to all of you who have watched and supported us along the ride. We have the most loyal fans on TV and we appreciate every one of you. You are what made doing this so fun.”

Life after Little People Big World

Jeremy wasted no time in getting back in the game after leaving Little People, Big World. In the same year, he developed a website dedicated to improving Christian marriages. Along with that, he also started a new podcast called Behind the Scenes with his wife, where they share their insights and stories about marriage, faith, family and entrepreneurship. On their website, theroloffs.com, they also sell their products and upload blog posts. Moreover, they have published two bestselling books: A Love Letter Life: Pursue Creatively, Date Intentionally, Love Faithfully and Creative Love: 10 Ways to Build a Fun and Lasting Love.

Net Worth

Jeremy has an estimated net worth of $700,000 and makes money from writing books, hosting podcasts, selling products, owning properties, and doing photography.

Marriage and Kids

Jeremy Roloff is happily married to his wife Audrey Mirabella ‘Auj’ Botti. The pair tied the knot in 2014 and have three lovely children. Ember Jean was born in September of 2017, Bode James was born on the 8th of January 2020, and Radley Knight Roloff was born on 8th of November, 2021.
